Just Encrypt - the right way to share secrets

  1. Get the Lock from the recipient and load it the "Browse" button.
  2. Type your secret into the "Unencrypted" text box and click on the "Encrypt" button.
  3. Copy the contents of the "Encrypted" text box and send it to the recipient.
  1. Get the encrypted content from the sender and put it into the "Encrypted" text box.
  2. Click on the "Decrypt" button and you will see the secret in the "Unencrypted" text box.
