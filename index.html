Just Encrypt - the right way to share secrets

If you don't have a key pair yet:

  1. Click on "Generate keys" to generate your pair of private and public keys.
  2. Do not share your private key with anyone!
  3. Share your public key with someone who needs to send you a secret securely.

How to send a secret to someone:

  1. Get the public key from the recipient and put it into the "Public Key" text area.
  2. Type your secret into the "Unencrypted" text box and click on the "Encrypt" button.
  3. Copy the contents of the "Encrypted" text box and send it to the recipient.

How to receive a secret from someone:

  1. Get the encrypted content from the sender and put it into the "Encrypted" text box.
  2. Put your private key into the "Private Key" text area.
  3. Click on the "Decrypt" button and you will see the secret in the "Unencrypted" text box.
