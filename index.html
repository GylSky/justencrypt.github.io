Just Encrypt - the right way to share secrets

  1. Get the Lock file from the recipient and click on the "Load recipient Lock file" button.
  2. Type your secret message into the text box and click on the "Lock" button.
  3. The secret message will be locked (random-looking characters).
  4. Send this locked message to the recipient.
  1. Download your Lock file and share it with the sender.
  2. Get the locked message from the sender and put it into the text box.
  3. Click on the "Unlock" button and you will see the original message.
  1. Click "Give me a new Key and Lock" to get a new set.
  2. Do not share your key with anyone else.
